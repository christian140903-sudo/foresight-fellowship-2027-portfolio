Short profile

I am an independent AI systems builder. Over the last years I built local agent infrastructure: remote phone/WhatsApp control, a Dual-Brain execution/evaluation pattern, Miguel Core as a local control plane, multi-model routing, memory governance, voice interfaces and an experimental cognitive architecture kernel.

Track fit

My strongest fit is Secure AI and secure multiagent systems. I have practical experience with the problems that appear when models do not only answer, but act through tools, memory, voice, remote channels and local systems.

Why Foresight

Foresight would help me move from an unusually broad personal laboratory into a more reproducible, mentored and technically disciplined project. I want to turn my prototypes into secure-agent evaluation and governance work.

Project answer

I want to build proof-of-concept work that connects agentic AI infrastructure with uncertainty, audit and validation logic. As an applied target I would use ECSA: a probabilistic AMR architecture that models resistance, uncertainty and stability and can start safely on synthetic/public data.

What support unlocks

Mentorship, technical review, networks, evaluation discipline and access to people who can test Secure AI, AI-for-science, governance and high-stakes validation seriously.
